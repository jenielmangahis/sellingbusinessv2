== Description ==

<a href="https://sendpulse.com/features/webpush">SendPulse Web
  Push</a> is a Wordpress plugin to add webpush functionality to your
  site. SendPulse sends over 150 mln webpush notifications every month
  for over 20,000 clients.

  Web push notifications are the instant notifications to your
  subscribers’ devices. An ideal solution to quickly inform your
  customers about news, sales, order status, or other important
  events.

  After plugin setup, your site visitors may sign-up to receive
  notifications when you publish new posts or just want to inform them
  about something important.

  Features:

  <ol>
    <li><b>Browsers supported</b>: Chrome (Desktop & Android), Safari
   (Mac OS X), and Firefox (Desktop) on both HTTP and HTTPS sites
   <li><b>Offline notifications</b>: Users will see your notification
   as soon as they come online
   <li><b>Personalization and segmentation</b>: Users at your site can
   receive personalized notifications. The messages you send out can
   be segmented based on the user’s location or other details.
   <li><b>A/B Testing</b>: Maximize the CTR of web push campaign by
   making a split test
   <li><b>Trigger messages and bulk messages</b>: Send push messages
   through your personal account or set up automatic messaging when
   certain conditions are met
   <li><b>Real Time Stats</b>: See your notifications being
   delivered in real time, geography stats, open and click rates and
   more
   <li><<b>Developer API</b>: Use SendPulse API and send web push
   notifications, emails, and SMS through a single platform.
  </ol>

  <b>Free. Everything is completely free. No strings attached.</b>

  Send as many as you need. Everything is completely free. No any
  fees, contracts and limitations.

Installation is plain and simple
