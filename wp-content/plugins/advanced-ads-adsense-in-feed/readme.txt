Display AdSense In-feed ads between posts.

== Description ==

This plugin is an add-on for the also free [Advanced Ads](https://wordpress.org/plugins/advanced-ads/) ad management plugin. It extends the plugin with a placement to put Google AdSense In-feed ads between posts on your homepage, archive pages, etc.

AdSense InFeed ads are a dedicated AdSense ad type to monetize overview pages with a highly engaging layout and specialized options.

Advanced Ads was developed to place, manage and test ads on pages to increase revenue and usability. 

The plugin comes with plenty of features, e.g., 

* Support for all AdSense types like link units, responsive, In-feed, In-article and matched content
* AdSense term violation checks
* AdSense Auto ads

[Full feature list](https://wpadvancedads.com/features/)

Advanced Ads Pro and other add-on features include

* ads on AMP pages (all ads, not only AdSense ads)
* ads based on specific browser widths
* showing an alternative ad to ad block users
* click fraud monitoring to protect your AdSense account
* position tests
* support for caching plugins
